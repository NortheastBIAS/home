About Me

The Northeast BioImage Analysts’ Society is a professional network dedicated to bioimage analysts working throughout the north-east region of the United States. As a group of passionate bioimage experts, we serve as a collaborative platform where bioimage analysts from different institutes in the region can connect, share knowledge, and promote the advancement of bioimage analysis.

Our mission is to foster a strong, supportive community for bioimage analysts by providing opportunities for networking, skill development, and the exchange of ideas. We aim to bridge gaps across institutions and disciplines, encouraging innovation and collaboration through. Our community-driven approach allows members to actively contribute to shaping the society’s future initiatives.

If you’re a bioimage analyst based in the northeast region, and are interested in joining our community of like-minded professionals, we welcome you to connect with us and get involved.
